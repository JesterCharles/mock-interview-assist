<objective>
Refactor middleware and server auth helpers to enumerate caller identity as `trainer | associate | anonymous` with strictly separate cookies. Middleware stays cookie-only (no DB). Server-component helpers (`isAssociateAuthenticated`, `getAssociateIdentity`, and the Phase-10-consumed `getAssociateSession`) perform the version check against `Associate.pinGeneratedAt` so regenerated PINs revoke outstanding cookies (D-09a, Codex finding #4).

Purpose: Prevents Pitfall 1 (single middleware conflating auth systems) and delivers the revocation path. This plan delivers AUTH-03 (per D-06, D-10..D-13) plus the version-check infrastructure.

Output: New `identity.ts`, extended `auth-server.ts` with version-aware associate helpers, refactored `middleware.ts`, tests proving trainer/associate separation AND stale-ver rejection.
</objective>

| request cookies → middleware | Cookies from client cross into identity resolution (cookie-only) |
| server-component helpers → DB | DB lookup for Associate.pinGeneratedAt version comparison (revocation gate) |
